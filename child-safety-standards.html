Child Safety & Reporting

6Clique is a social app designed for adults. We are committed to user safety, swift moderation, and compliance with platform policies. This page explains our 18+ age restriction, how to report content or accounts, and what happens after a report.

Age Requirement (18+ Only)

  • Minimum age: You must be at least 18 years old to use 6Clique. Users under 18 are not permitted.
  • Enforcement: If we learn that an account belongs to someone under 18, the account will be removed immediately.
  • Age checks: We may request additional verification if we have reason to believe an account is underage or otherwise in violation of our Terms.

How to Report Content or Accounts

You can report content or behavior that violates our guidelines directly in the app:

  • Profiles: Tap the flag button on a user’s profile.
  • Messages: Long-press a message and choose Report.
  • Journals & Comments: Tap the ⋯ (three-dot) button next to a journal or comment and select Report.

When reporting, please include any relevant details or context to help our moderators review quickly.

What We Review & Possible Actions

Our moderation team reviews reports as soon as possible. We take action on violations, including but not limited to:

  • Removing violating content (profiles, journals, comments, messages)
  • Temporary restrictions (read-only mode, feature limits)
  • Account suspension or permanent removal
  • Immediate removal of any account identified as belonging to someone under 18

Safety Tools

  • Block & Mute: You can block or mute other users to stop unwanted contact.
  • Privacy Controls: Adjust your visibility and interaction preferences in Settings.
  • In-App Reporting: Fast, built-in tools to report content or users from profiles, messages, journals, and comments.

Content Standards

To promote a safe environment, 6Clique prohibits content that includes (non-exhaustive):

  • Harassment, hate speech, or threats
  • Sexual content involving minors or any sexual exploitation
  • Graphic violence or encouragement of self-harm
  • Illegal activities or instructions that facilitate harm
  • Spam, scams, or deceptive practices

Emergency Situations

If someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services right away. You can still submit an in-app report; our team will handle it as a priority.

Data Handling for Safety

  • We use report details and related metadata to investigate and enforce our policies.
  • When we remove an under-18 account, it is deleted. Some minimal records may be retained for a limited period to meet legal, security, and anti-abuse obligations.
  • For more on how we handle data, please review our Privacy Policy.

Law-Enforcement Requests

We cooperate with valid legal requests and may preserve data when legally required. Non-emergency inquiries can be directed to support@6clique.com.

Contact & Support

If you have questions or need help, contact us at support@6clique.com.
