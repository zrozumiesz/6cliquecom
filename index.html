How It Works

6Clique connects you with five other people based on your emotional alignment in a small, safe group where you can share honestly and feel truly known. For six days, you answer one thoughtful reflection each day. At night, your group's answers are shared anonymously among one another. This is where real conversation begins. No swiping. No endless scrolling. No small talk. Just real conversations that matter.
