Frequently Asked Questions

Is 6Clique free to use?

Yes, users can download and enjoy the core 6Clique experience at no cost! Free users can:

  • Post one public journal per day and unlimited private journals.
  • Participate in at least one active group chat of six members at all times.
  • Access daily reflection prompts and detailed insights into your own emotional alignment.
  • Engage with the community through comments and likes.
  • Earn 2 leaves a day through answering daily reflections and journaling, unlocking premium features such as anonymous journals, deeper emotional insights about others, and 1:1 conversations.

Basic Premium Plan: Upgrade to 60 leaves per month for $4.99 and explore more features, deeper insights, and greater connection opportunities.

6Clique is created and maintained by a single independent developer, without outside funding. Premium plans help keep our space ad-free, safe, and steadily growing.

Download now and join small groups for free — premium features are optional!

How do daily reflections work?

If you’ve opted into notifications, you’ll receive a new daily reflection question at 6 AM Central Time. You have until 6 PM Central to respond (500-character limit).

At 8 PM Central, our server gathers all responses from your group members and posts them—anonymously and in random order—into each group chat you’re part of, bundled into a single message.

If a particular anonymous response resonates with you, you can spend 3 leaves to reveal the author and connect more personally. This often sparks deeper, more meaningful conversations within the group.

Can I leave a group at any time?
Yes — you’re always in control of your participation. You can leave or end your session at any time without disrupting others. Messages you’ve already sent will remain visible until the group naturally expires (6 days after it starts). Twenty days after the group closes, all related group data is permanently deleted from our servers.
Who can see my journals?

Share your thoughts in three ways: Public, Anonymous, or Private.

  • Public: Your journal is linked to your profile, visible to everyone in the community.
  • Private: Only you can see it — completely secure and never accessible by others. RLS rules ensure no one else can access them in any way. You are, however, responsible for keeping your own credentials secure.
  • Anonymous: Your words are shared without showing your identity. If someone resonates with your entry, they can spend 5 leaves to connect with you personally.
How do you keep the community safe from harassment?

You can report any content that feels unsafe or inappropriate at any time. This includes profiles (tap the flag icon at the top right), messages (long-press), or comments and journal entries (use the three-dot menu). You can also block specific users to prevent them from contacting you. Our moderators review reports promptly, usually within 7 days. If harmful behavior is confirmed, the user may receive a warning or have their account removed to keep the community safe.
